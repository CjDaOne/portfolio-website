Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

1. Overview and Corporate Structure

This Privacy Policy describes how CJNF Solutions LLC ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, processes, and protects information gathered through our corporate website and standard administrative operations. This policy explicitly governs data collected directly by CJNF Solutions LLC during consultations, discovery processing, and the deployment of our managed product line, CJNF Systems.

2. Mobile Carrier & SMS Compliance Mandate

In strict accordance with telecommunications carrier networks and A2P 10DLC regulatory standards, the following data isolation guidelines apply to mobile numbers collected through any intake channel:

"Mobile Sharing Compliance: No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties."

3. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when submitting an Operations Discovery Profile or initializing system provisioning, including:

  • Identity Parameters: Full name, corporate title, business entity registration name.
  • Contact Coordinates: Business email address, active mobile or direct telephone numbers.
  • Operational Metrics: Baseline business revenue volumes, process architecture descriptions, and primary operational bottlenecks.

4. Data Controller vs. Data Processor Realization

It is functionally critical to differentiate our varying positions relative to client data management:

  • Data Controller: CJNF Solutions LLC operates strictly as a Data Controller regarding information gathered on our primary single-page domain or via direct intake structures used to build internal client files.
  • Data Processor: When we build, integrate, and deploy a managed CJNF Systems environment for a client's business, CJNF Solutions LLC operates solely as a Data Processor. The client entity remains the Data Controller for their respective customer, consumer, and workflow information processed by that dedicated system.

5. How We Use Information

Collected metrics and identifiers are utilized strictly for the following execution lines:

  • Provisioning, tuning, and validating dedicated pipeline architectures and system deployments.
  • Transmitting system operational logs, text notifications, and structural updates regarding your setup.
  • Maintaining corporate compliance, auditing active workflows, and defending network integrity.

6. Contact and Administration

For inquiries regarding this privacy framework, data access rights, or technical opt-out executions, contact our compliance administrator directly at: contact@cjnfsolutions.com.
