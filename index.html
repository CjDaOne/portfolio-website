Composite Photography

Composite Photography is the blending of two or more images to create one final image. While the definition makes Compositing sound quick and easy, it's anything but, often times requiring several hours of photography and Photoshop work to complete a final piece.

Photo Restoration

Photograph restoration is the art of restoring the appearance of a digital copy of a physical photograph which has been damaged by natural, man made, or environmental causes or affected by age or neglect.

Web Development

Full Stack Web Development/Engineering is multidisciplinary and encompasses contributions from diverse areas: design and systems analysis, software engineering, hypermedia/hypertext engineering, user interface, information indexing and retrieval, testing, modelling and simulation, project management, and graphic design and presentation. While Full Stack Web Development uses software engineering principles, it encompasses new approaches, methodologies, tools, techniques, and guidelines to meet the unique requirements of Web-based applications.
